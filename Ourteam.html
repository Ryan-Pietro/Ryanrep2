MEET THE TEAM AND ASTRONAUTS OF NASA

Welcome to NASA's vibrant community of scientists, engineers, astronauts, and innovators who are pushing the boundaries of human exploration and scientific discovery. Here, you'll discover the diverse teams and remarkable individuals who make up the backbone of NASA's pioneering missions and groundbreaking research efforts.

Exploration Teams

Space Exploration Division: Dive into the world of space exploration with NASA's Space Exploration Division, which encompasses missions to the Moon, Mars, and beyond. From developing next-generation spacecraft to planning human missions to distant destinations, this team is at the forefront of humanity's quest to explore the cosmos.

Planetary Science Division: Explore the wonders of our solar system and beyond with NASA's Planetary Science Division, which studies planets, moons, asteroids, and comets to unlock the secrets of their formation, evolution, and potential for life.

Astrophysics Division: Peer into the depths of the universe with NASA's Astrophysics Division, which explores the cosmos through telescopes, spacecraft, and cutting-edge technologies to study black holes, galaxies, stars, and the origins of the universe.

Aeronautics Research Division: Soar through the skies with NASA's Aeronautics Research Division, which advances the science and technology of flight to improve aviation safety, efficiency, and environmental sustainability.

Meet the Astronaut Corps:

Commander Jessica Watkins: Learn about Commander Jessica Watkins, a geologist, and NASA astronaut, who is preparing for future missions to the Moon and Mars as part of the Artemis program. With a background in planetary science and field research, Commander Watkins brings a unique perspective to space exploration. Dr. Serena Auñón-Chancellor: Discover Dr. Serena Auñón-Chancellor, a physician, engineer, and NASA astronaut, who has conducted research aboard the International Space Station (ISS) and is training for future missions to explore the Moon and beyond. Dr. Auñón-Chancellor's expertise in medicine and engineering is invaluable to NASA's efforts to support human spaceflight. Commander Victor Glover: Meet Commander Victor Glover, a pilot, engineer, and NASA astronaut, who recently completed a mission aboard the ISS as part of Expedition 64/65. Commander Glover's experience in aviation and engineering is instrumental to NASA's plans for future crewed missions to the Moon, Mars, and beyond.
